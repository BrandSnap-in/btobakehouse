FAQ

Common questions.

Do you offer samples before a first order?

+

Yes — every new wholesale account gets a sample batch matched to their product interest before confirming a full order.

Can recipes be adjusted for allergies or preferences?

+

Within reason, yes. Tell us about allergen or ingredient constraints during the spec-and-sample stage, before the recipe is locked for your account.

Do you deliver outside Delhi NCR?

+

Our standard delivery coverage is Delhi NCR. For larger standing orders outside this radius, get in touch and we'll confirm feasibility.

Can I set up a recurring/standing order?

+

Yes — standing orders are the default setup for most café and hotel accounts once the first batch is approved.

What packaging do you use for bulk items?

+

Packaging is matched to product and handling needs — sealed pouches for chocolate, food-grade tubs for ganache, and insulated packing for chilled items. Private-label packaging is available for standing accounts.
