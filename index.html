What we stand on

Three commitments on every order ticket.

These aren't slogans — they're the three things we check before a batch leaves the kitchen.

01 / CRAFT

Made the slow way

Chocolate is tempered, ganache is rested, and mousse is layered by hand — the same way a pastry kitchen would do it for its own dining room, just at wholesale volume.

02 / PRECISION

Every batch, to spec

Weight, sweetness, and finish are fixed to a written spec per SKU, so the brownie you order in March tastes identical in November.

Built for repeat orders

Standing orders, seasonal spikes, event volumes — production is planned around your calendar, not the other way around.
