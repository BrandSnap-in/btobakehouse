A kitchen built to hold a spec, batch after batch.

BtoBakehouse exists to solve one problem for food businesses: getting café-quality chocolate and baked goods at the volume a business actually needs, without the quality dropping off after the first order.

The story

A childhood love for baking, now built to scale.

BtoBakehouse is the wholesale arm of Mélange by Vasavi, founded by Chef Vasavi — a Le Cordon Bleu, London alumna whose love of baking started at age eight, picking cookbooks off her parents' shelf and baking for family friends' birthdays and events long before it was a career.

That path led through a pastry role in Sydney, a diploma in patisserie and boulangerie at Le Cordon Bleu London, and finally Mélange by Vasavi — a home-kitchen operation that grew into a trusted Delhi NCR patisserie for cakes, breads, hampers and catering. BtoBakehouse carries that same training and standards into bulk supply, so cafés, hotels and resellers get wholesale volume without losing the craft it started with.

How we work

Three pillars, in practice.

CRAFT

Hand-made methods

Tempering, ganache-resting, and mousse-layering are done by hand at every batch size — we don't switch to shortcuts once volume goes up.

PRECISION

Spec sheets, not guesswork

Every SKU has a fixed recipe, weight and finish. New batches are checked against the original sample before they ship.

SCALE

Planned around your order

Production runs are scheduled against your delivery calendar, so standing orders and seasonal spikes are planned for, not scrambled for.

Ingredients & sourcing

What goes into the batch.

We work with couverture-grade chocolate, fresh dairy, and seasonal fruit sourced for consistency, not just cost. Ingredient swaps are only made with prior sign-off from wholesale partners — never silently.

Couverture chocolate Fresh dairy No filler bulking Batch-tested recipes
Handling & delivery

Getting it to you fresh.

Chilled items travel in insulated, sealed transport; ambient items are packed to survive café and hotel back-of-house handling. Delivery windows are agreed per account so stock lands when your kitchen is ready for it.

Insulated chilled transport Scheduled delivery windows Wholesale-grade packaging
